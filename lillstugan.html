Lillstugan

Lillstugan is our coziest cabin — perfect for a peaceful and private getaway. Compact in size but generous in atmosphere, it offers a quiet retreat for guests who want to slow down and enjoy the surroundings. The combined living and sleeping space includes a comfortable double bed and a small sitting area, while the kitchenette provides all essentials for simple, home-cooked meals. With its intimate feel and charming details, Lillstugan is ideal for solo travelers or couples looking for calm, comfort, and a touch of countryside magic.
