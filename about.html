Sjöstugan

Nestled on the shores of beautiful Lake Siljan in the heart of Dalarna, our bed and breakfast is a family-owned retreat built on tradition, hospitality, and a genuine love for this region. What began as a small family project has grown into a place where guests can slow down, breathe deeply, and experience the calm and charm of Swedish countryside living.
We take pride in offering a personal touch — from the homemade breakfasts inspired by local flavors to the thoughtfully designed cabins and rooms that reflect the character of Dalarna. Whether you’re here to explore the surrounding forests, enjoy the peaceful lake views, or simply unwind in nature, we want you to feel at home from the moment you arrive.
For us, this is more than a business. It’s a place where families gather, friends reconnect, and lasting memories are made. We look forward to welcoming you to our little corner of Siljan. Book Your Stay Now →
